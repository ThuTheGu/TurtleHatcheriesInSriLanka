Introduction To Turtles

Turtles are one of the most important and endangered animals in Sri Lanka. Many tourists are coming to Sri Lanka to see turtles. There are many species of turtles live in Sri Lanka. Some examples are Green turtle,Olive ridley and Hawksbill. Turtles are one of the main reasons why the tourists from many countries are coming to Sri Lanka. Turtles are very useful to increase our tourists attractions and that will help to increase our economy as a country.Turtles are one of the most important sea creatures in the world.In past in sri lanka there are many turtle habitat in Sri Lanka.But nowadays the turtle habitat is shrinking due to many causes.Examples are Man made causes and natural causes.So as Sri Lankans we must protect our turtle habitat.


There are reasons why turtles loss their habitat.Man made causes are one of the main reasons why turtles lost their habitat.And also natural disasters like Tsunamis are Natural causes like climate change also play a main roll in turtle habitat loss. So to protect the turtle habitat we can do many things.Reduce garbage and debris from the ocean, Reglarly attend and participate in coastal clean-ups,Use re-usable bags,Do not not hurt the baby tortles and eggs, Turtle conservation projects and One of the main turtle habitat protective way is Turtle Hatcheries.Turtle Hatcheries provide good and successfull way to increase the turtle habitat.



Importance Of Turtles

Turtles are one of the important animals in the wild world.Turtles are also very important in the sea and also in the coastal areas.As a marine creature Turtle are very impoetant to marine life.

As humans we will get lot of adavantages from turtles.In Sri Lanka Turtles are one of the most important tourist attractions.Many Tourists are come to Sri Lanka to see Turtles.Here are some of the main importance of turtles:

  1. nesting helps beaches
  2. Turtles help control their prey
  3. Turtles are important to coastal communities
  4. Turtles are important to native communities
  5. Hatchlings are important food for many animals

Turtle Hatcheries Functions

Turtle Hatcheries are the main way to protect turtle habitat.In Sri Lanka there are many turtle hatchries around the country,Examples are Kosgoda,Hikkaduwa and Bentota.Hatcheries are responsible for many duties.

Turtle hatchries are responsible for protect the turtle habitat in Sri Lanka.They use many maethods to increase turtle habitat.There are many functions related to turtle hatcheries.Here are some of the functions of Turtle Hatcheries:

  • Making artificial nesting grounds
  • Managing artificial nesting grounds
  • Protect turtle eggs
  • Protect baby turtle
  • Increasing turtle habitat

Reasons To Protect Turtles?

Turtles are vey important to the environment. In marine world turtles are very important animal. Turtles support the coastal areas to be more beautiful and clean.Tutrles are also very important to human.

To protect the balance of the environment turtles do main tasks. As for Sri Lanka Tourism industry are also depending on Turtle habitat in Sri Lanka. Here are some reasons to protect turtle habitat:

  1. Maintaining food web balance in the ocean
  2. Critical for biodiversity in the ocean
  3. Coastal communities have many benefits from turtles
  4. Native communities have many advatages from turtles
  5. Turtle nesting helps beaches
