1. Green Turtles

Green turtle is most common category of turtle in Sri Lanka.They are name as "Green Turtle" because of their colour.They can weight up to 200kg.They mostly eat sea grass and algae.Green turtles are mostly found in the southern coast,the eastern coast and the west coast.

2. Olive Ridley Turtle

The smallest turtle found in Sri Lanka.They name as "Olive Ridley Turtle" because of their olive coloured shell.They can be weight up to 50kg.They are vulnerable species.They are mostly vulnerable to human activities.

3. Loggerhead Turtle

Large turtle found in Sri Lanka.They can be weight up to 280kg.They name as "Loggerhead Turtle" because of their large head.They are critically endangered turtle category in Sri Lanka.

4. Hawksbill Turtle

They name as "Hawksbill Turtle" because of their narrow and pointed beak.They can be weight up to 80kg.They are critically endangered turtle category in Sri Lanka. They are mostly found in the southern coast,the eastern coast and the west coast.
