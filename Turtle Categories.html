Turtle Categories In Sri Lanka

Sri Lanka is one of the beautiful countries in the world.Many tourists are coming to Sri Lanka to see the beauty of the island country.Sri Lanka is one of the hotspots for the turtle habitat in the world.There are 5 categories of turtles live in Sri lanka.They are Green turtle,Olive ridely turtle,Hawksbill turtle,Leatherback turtle and Loggerhead turtle.Those categories of turtles live around the Sri Lanka in many coastal ragions.They live happily and freely in the coastal areas in Sri Lanka.So we must the turtle habitat of Sri Lanka.Turtles are one of the most beautiful animals in the world.By having lots of types and categories the beauty of biodiversity of turtle habitat is increasing.So as humans we must protect the turtle habitat.Here are some important details about the five categories of turtles live in Sri lanka:

1. Green Turtles

Green turtle is most common category of turtle in Sri Lanka.They are name as "Green Turtle" because of their colour.They can weight up to 200kg.They mostly eat sea grass and algae.Green turtles are mostly found in the southern coast,the eastern coast and the west coast.

2. Olive Ridley Turtle

The smallest turtle found in Sri Lanka.They name as "Olive Ridley Turtle" because of their olive coloured shell.They can be weight up to 50kg.They are vulnerable species.They are mostly vulnerable to human activities.

3. Loggerhead Turtle

Large turtle found in Sri Lanka.They can be weight up to 280kg.They name as "Loggerhead Turtle" because of their large head.They are critically endangered turtle category in Sri Lanka.

4. Hawksbill Turtle

They name as "Hawksbill Turtle" because of their narrow and pointed beak.They can be weight up to 80kg.They are critically endangered turtle category in Sri Lanka. They are mostly found in the southern coast,the eastern coast and the west coast.

5. Leatherback Turtle

Largest sea turtle.It can weight up to 900kg.They are named because of their leathery skin.Rare in Sri Lanka.Mainly found in Sri Lanka's northern waters.
