Threats To Turtles

Turtles are one of the most important animlas in the world.In past there is a high turtle habitat in the world.But due to many reasons turtle habitat in the world and in Sri lanka are decreasing.That is becoming a huge loss for environment and for biodiversity.So we must protect our turtle habitat.We can do many things to protect our turtle habitat.Some examples are coastal clean-ups,turtle hatcheires and turtle conservation projects.So by doing this kind of things we can protect and increase our turtle habitat.


There are many threats to the turtles.Those are can be human threats or natural threats.But nowadays human therats overtake natural threats.That because many man made projects are developing in coastal regions.So that becoming a huge risk for turtle habitat.So we must protect our turtle habitat.Here are some threats to turtles:


  1. Over harvesting and illegal trade
  2. Habitat loss
  3. Climate change
  4. Pollution

1. Over Harvesting And Illegal Trade

Over harvesting and illegal trading is now becoming one of the main threats to turtles.Turtles are continued to be harvested unsuctainably.Many people do that for get meat and eggs as a food source.Some killing turtles for medicines and religious ceremonies.Because of those reasons turtle habitat decreasing annually.So we must take legal actions and we must protect turtle habitat.

2. Habitat Loss

Turtles make their nests on beaches.But due to unorganized coastal developments and vehicle traffic those turtle nests are destroying everyday.Many human activities in coastal regions are destroying turtle nests bescause of that female turtles can not lay their eggs.So then turtle habitat will decrease rapidly.So we must protect coastal regions and turtle nests to increase the turtle habitat.




Solutions/Conversations

3. Climate Change

Climate change plays a critical role in turtle habitat loss.Climate conditions like temperature is very important to turtles.Warm temperature will affect turtle's behaviours.Severe storms,Tsunamis and sea level rising also affect the nesting of turtles.So as humans we protect our environment to increase and protect turtle habitat.






Solutions/Conversations

4. Pollution

Polythenes,plastics and rubbish are the main pollutants that are serious threats to turtles.Turtles eat those things are because those things do not digest they simply dying.Oil spill also a main threat to turtles.Most oil spills are caused by ship accidents.Oil are heavily poisioned to turtles and turtles are dying including baby turtles. So we must stop throwing polythenes,plastics and rubbish to the sea and to the beach.We can organize coastal clean-ups.We can implement new rules to protect our coastal regions and out turtle habital.By then we can increase out turtle habitat and protect our turtle habitat.
