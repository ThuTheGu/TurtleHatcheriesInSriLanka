There are many turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka.One of them is Bentota turtle hatchery.It is locate in Bentota.Many tourists and local citizens visit that place.It is one of the main tourist attractions in Sri Lanka.



Bentota turtle hatchery is responsible for increasing turtle habitat of Sri Lanka and protect Turtle habitat in Sri lanka.Bentota turtle hatchery use many methods and techniques to increase and protect turtle habitat in Sri Lanka.

1. Bentota Turtle Hatchery


Bentota turtle hatchery established to increase and protect turtle habitat in Sri Lanka. Bentota turtle hatchery located in Southern province,Galle district.Bentota turtle hatchery center is locate in Bentota.Many tourists and locals come to see this turtle hatchery.Now it is becoming a tourist attraction.


2. Bentota Turtle Hatchery Functions


As a turtle hatchery Bentota turtle hatchery used many methods and techniques to increase the turtle habitat in Sri Lanka and protect turtle habitat in Sri Lanka.Artificial nesting ground development,artificial nesting ground management,Conservation projects,coastal clean-ups,protecting and conserving turtle eggs,protecting baby turtles and protecting and curing injured turtles are some of the main functions in Bentota turtle hatchery.
